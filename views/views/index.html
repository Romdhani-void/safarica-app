Safarica Travel Agency, founded in 2024, is an emerging leader in the Tunisian tourism industry, employing a team of 400 professionals. Handling over 1.2 million passengers annually, Safarica partners with major global tour operators to offer top-tier travel services across Tunisia's most sought-after destinations. With a presence in all 8 international airports and branches located in key tourist hubs, Safarica ensures smooth logistics and exceptional customer service. In addition to its physical ticketing agency in downtown Tunis, Safarica offers a user-friendly online booking platform, Safarica Booking, making travel planning effortless for both domestic and international travelers.
